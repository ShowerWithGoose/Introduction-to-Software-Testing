**Summary**

The primary logical error is located in the `next()` method of `frontend/Lexer.java`. After incrementing `this.pos`, the code repeatedly accesses `this.line.charAt(this.pos)` or uses `this.pos` in substring operations **without first verifying that `this.pos < this.line.length()`**. This will cause a `StringIndexOutOfBoundsException` whenever a digit, multi-character operator, comment, string/char literal, or identifier appears at the very end of the input file.
