**Summary of the Issue:**
The program contains a critical logical error in the post-order traversal loop within `Compiler.java`. The `Queue.poll()` method is invoked twice during each iteration: first to assign the current element to the variable `s`, and a second time inside the `if` condition to append to the `StringBuilder`. This causes every other token/syntax component to be skipped from the output and can lead to incorrect results or a `NullPointerException` when the queue becomes empty unexpectedly. The second `poll()` call should simply use the already retrieved variable `s`.
