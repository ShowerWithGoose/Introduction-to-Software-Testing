**Summary of the Error:**
The program fails to compile due to a **variable shadowing issue** in `Compiler.java`. The class-level static field `lexer` (a `String` representing the output filename `"lexer.txt"`) is shadowed by a local variable `lexer` (a `Lexer` object) declared inside the `main` method. Consequently, the call `output.output(lexer, TokenList.getTokenList().toString())` attempts to pass a `Lexer` object to the `output` method, which strictly expects a `String` filename. This results in a type mismatch compilation error (`incompatible types: Lexer cannot be converted to String`).
