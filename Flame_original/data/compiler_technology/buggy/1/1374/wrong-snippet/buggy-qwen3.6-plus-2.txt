Based on the problem description and the compilation error indicated by the test case, the primary issue lies in `Compiler.java`. There is a **variable shadowing** problem: the local variable `Lexer lexer` inside the `main` method shadows the static field `String lexer`. As a result, the call `output.output(lexer, ...)` attempts to pass a `Lexer` object to a method that expects a `String` filename, causing a type mismatch compilation error.
