### Summary of Errors
The program contains logical errors in the output of syntax component names, which will cause incorrect output formatting and duplicate entries:
1. **Duplicate `<Stmt>` Output**: `parseStmt()` calls `outputGrammar("<Stmt>")` at the end, but all the specific statement parsing helper methods (e.g., `parseEmptyStmt()`, `parseIfStmt()`, `parseReturnStmt()`, etc.) also call `outputGrammar("<Stmt>")` internally. This results in `<Stmt>` being printed twice for every statement.
2. **Multiple Outputs in Expression Parsing Loops**: Methods like `parseRelExp()`, `parseAddExp()`, `parseMulExp()`, `parseLOrExp()`, `parseLAndExp()`, and `parseEqExp()` incorrectly call `outputGrammar()` inside their `while` loops. According to the requirement, a non-terminal syntax component should only be output **once** after its entire structure has been fully parsed. The current implementation outputs the component name repeatedly during iterative parsing.
