1. The condition `!words.get(pos + 2).getStr().equals("(")` may cause out-of-bounds access
2. The condition `!words.get(pos + 1).getStr().equals("main")` similarly may cause issues
3. The parsing logic doesn't properly handle the grammar's structure where CompUnit can have any number of Decl and FuncDef before MainFuncDef
