**Summary of the Issue:**
The program implements a recursive descent parser for the given grammar. While the overall structure follows the grammar rules, there is a critical logical error in the handling of left-recursive logical expression rules (`LAndExp` and `LOrExp`). The `while` loop conditions incorrectly include tokens that can start an expression (`LPARENT`, `IDENFR`, `INTCON`, `CHRCON`, `PLUS`, `MINU`, `NOT`) alongside the logical operators. This causes the parser to enter the loop and consume subsequent expressions even when the `&&` or `||` operator is absent, leading to incorrect syntax tree construction, masked syntax errors, or infinite parsing loops. The loop conditions should strictly check for `AND` and `OR` respectively. Additionally, the `ChangeFellow` function contains unsafe array indexing that may cause segmentation faults during tree restructuring.
