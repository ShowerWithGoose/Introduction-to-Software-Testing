### Summary
The lexical analyzer is designed to report errors with their corresponding line numbers. However, the `readlines()` method in `readfile.java` explicitly filters out empty lines (`if(linenow.length()!=0)`). This causes the internal line counter (`now_line_num`) to fall out of sync with the actual line numbers in the source file. As a result, when an error token is created and later output in `Compiler.java`, the reported line number will be incorrect. To maintain accurate line numbering, all lines (including empty ones) must be stored in the `contents` list.
