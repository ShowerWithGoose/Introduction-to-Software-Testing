**Summary of the Issue:**
The program correctly identifies the lexical error for individual `&` or `|` symbols and writes the error information to `error.txt`. However, after detecting the error, it unconditionally continues execution and calls `printToLexer()`. Since `tokenType` remains initialized to `"NULL"` for erroneous single-character operators, the program incorrectly outputs `NULL &` or `NULL |` to `lexer.txt`. The logic should either skip the lexer output for erroneous tokens or return immediately after reporting the error to prevent invalid token emission.
