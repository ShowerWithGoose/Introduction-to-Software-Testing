**Summary of the Issue:**
The lexical analyzer incorrectly handles the error case for single `&` and `|` symbols (error type `a`). In the `addComparatorToken` method, when a single `&` or `|` is encountered, the code correctly detects the error, records it, and adds the single-character token. However, due to a missing `else` keyword, the execution falls through unconditionally to the next line, which incorrectly adds the double-character token (`&&` or `||`) as well. This results in duplicate/incorrect tokens being output for erroneous inputs.
