The error is likely in the `getChar()` method. When the input ends with a newline character, the method does not properly handle the transition to the end-of-file state. Specifically, when `pos` reaches `input.length() - 1` and the character at that position is `'\n'`, the method increments `line` but then sets `curChar` to `'\0'` and increments `pos` to `input.length()`. However, the `getSymbol()` method's main loop checks `while (curChar == ' ' || curChar == '\n' || curChar == '\t')` and calls `getChar()` again, which may cause issues when `pos` is already at `input.length()`. This can lead to incorrect line number tracking for errors occurring at the end of the file.
