### Summary
The provided program fails to compile/run correctly, as indicated by the `javac: not found` / Compilation Error message. Upon reviewing the source code, a critical semantic/logical error is found in the `operatorsmap` initialization within `CustomCLexer.java`. The multiplication operator `*` is incorrectly mapped to `Tokentype.BREAKTK` instead of `Tokentype.MULT`. This mismatch will cause incorrect token generation for `*` and likely trigger downstream compilation or runtime failures during testing. Additionally, the error handling for single `&` and `|` correctly identifies the lexical error type `a`, but the primary bug lies in the operator mapping.
