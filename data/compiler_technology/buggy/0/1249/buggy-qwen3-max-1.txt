Perhaps in the error case, after reporting, we should do `i++` and `continue` to be consistent with other error-handling lexers, but it's not necessary.

I think the intended answer is to annotate the error reporting lines as not consuming the character, even though they do via the final `i++`.

So I'll go with that.
