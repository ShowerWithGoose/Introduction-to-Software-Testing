**Summary of the Issue:**
The program incorrectly reports the line number for lexical error type `a` (individual `&` or `|` symbols). In the `nextSymbol()` method, `getChar()` is called to peek at the next character. If `&` or `|` appears at the end of a line, `getChar()` reads the newline character (`\n`) and increments the `line` counter *before* `errorLine` is assigned. Consequently, `errorLine = line;` captures the line number of the *next* line instead of the line where the error actually occurred. This off-by-one error causes the output in `error.txt` to fail the test cases.
