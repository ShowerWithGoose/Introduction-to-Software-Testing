However, analyzing the source code reveals a significant logical error in `Compiler.java` regarding the handling of cross-line comments (`/* ... */`). This causes the `*/` characters to be incorrectly processed as tokens (`MULT` and `DIV`) or causes the remainder of the line after the comment to be completely skipped. Additionally, the `&` and `|` error detection in `Lexer.java` has a minor fall-through issue where `setType()` is called unnecessarily after an error is flagged, though it doesn't break functionality due to the priority of the error flag check.
