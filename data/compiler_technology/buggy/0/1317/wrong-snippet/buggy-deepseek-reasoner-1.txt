The errors are:
1. In `parseIdent`, the while condition lacks parentheses around `(isIdent(line.charAt(pos)) || isDigit(line.charAt(pos)))`, causing an `IndexOutOfBoundsException` when the last character in the line is a digit or when the line has only a single identifier character (due to short-circuit evaluation). The corrected condition should be `while (pos < line.length() && (isIdent(line.charAt(pos)) || isDigit(line.charAt(pos))))`.

2. In `parseSign`, the `case '/'` is missing a `break` statement, causing it to fall through to the `default` case. This results in the '/' character being processed twice (once in the case and once in default), leading to an extra token or incorrect behavior for comments. A `break;` should be added after the `else` block.
