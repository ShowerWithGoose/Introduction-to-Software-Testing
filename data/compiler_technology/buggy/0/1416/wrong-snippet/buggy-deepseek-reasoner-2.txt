The error is that after incrementing `pos` to the next character, the code does not check whether `pos` is within bounds before accessing `line.charAt(pos)`. When the character `&` or `|` appears at the end of the line, this causes a `StringIndexOutOfBoundsException`, resulting in an empty output instead of reporting the lexical error.
