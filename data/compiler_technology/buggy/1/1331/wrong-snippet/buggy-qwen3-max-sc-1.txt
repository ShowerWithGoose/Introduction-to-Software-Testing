Based on the problem description and code review, the main issue appears to be in the lexical analysis mapping table where the brace/bracket mappings are incorrect. Specifically in the `lexMap` and `LexTypeNames` arrays, the curly braces `{}` and square brackets `[]` are mapped to wrong token types.
