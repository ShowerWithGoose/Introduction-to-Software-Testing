The method incorrectly checks for the start of MainFuncDef using `tokens.get(curPos+1)` and `tokens.get(curPos+2)`, which can cause ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException when near the end of tokens. Additionally, the loop conditions don't properly handle cases where there are no Decl or FuncDef components before MainFuncDef.
