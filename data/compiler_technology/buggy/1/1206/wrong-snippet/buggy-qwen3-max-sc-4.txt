Based on the problem description and code review, the main issue appears to be in the `consumeToken` method in `Parser.java`, which handles error recovery for missing tokens but doesn't properly handle all cases. Specifically, the error handling for missing semicolons, right parentheses, and right brackets creates fake tokens but doesn't advance the parser correctly, leading to potential infinite loops or incorrect parsing.

The most critical error is in the `InitVal()` method in `Parser.java`, where the loop condition for parsing comma-separated expressions in braces is incorrect - it should check for `COMMA` tokens followed by expressions, not just continue until `RBRACE`.
