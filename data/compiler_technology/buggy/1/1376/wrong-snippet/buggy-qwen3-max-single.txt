These methods use a complex and error-prone approach with `peekReset`, `peekDisable`, and `peekEnable` that doesn't correctly handle operator precedence and associativity.

The most problematic area is in the **`parseAddExp` method** in `Parser.java`, which has similar issues in other expression parsing methods. The current implementation tries to parse left-associative operators but uses an incorrect backtracking mechanism that can lead to infinite loops or incorrect parsing.
