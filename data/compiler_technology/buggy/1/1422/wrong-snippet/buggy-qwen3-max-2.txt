Based on the problem description and code review, the main issue appears to be in the Parser.java file, specifically in the parse() method initialization and word processing logic. The key problems are:

1. The lexer processes all tokens upfront before parsing begins, but the parser's getWord() method tries to output the "lastWord" which may not be properly initialized
2. The initial setup of lastWord and curWord has ordering issues that can cause incorrect error line number reporting
