### Summary
The test environment reports a `Compilation Error` primarily due to `javac: not found`, but the source code itself contains several critical logical and runtime defects that would prevent successful execution or correct parsing:
1. **`frontend/Token.java`**: `String.format` has 2 `%s` placeholders but 3 arguments, which will throw a `MissingFormatArgumentException` at runtime.
2. **`frontend/Praser.java` (`CompUnit`)**: Accesses `tokenList.get(curIndex+1)` and `tokenList.get(curIndex+2)` without bounds checking, risking `IndexOutOfBoundsException`. Additionally, the `while` loop lacks an `else` break/error handling clause, causing an infinite loop if an unexpected token is encountered.
3. **`frontend/Lexer.java` (`getToken`)**: Checks `content.charAt(i+1)` without verifying `i+1 < content.length()`, leading to `StringIndexOutOfBoundsException` at the end of the file.
4. **`frontend/Praser.java` (`Stmt`)**: The `for` statement parsing logic incorrectly handles the optional `[ForStmt]` and `[Cond]` components, failing to match the grammar `'for' '(' [ForStmt] ';' [Cond] ';' [ForStmt] ')' Stmt`.
