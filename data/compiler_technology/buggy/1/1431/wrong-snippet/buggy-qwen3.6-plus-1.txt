### Summary
The program fails to compile primarily due to a **redefinition of `struct block`** in `all.h`. The struct is initially defined with a `std::vector<struct blockitem>` member, and later in the same file, it is redefined as an empty struct. This violates C++ compilation rules and directly causes the reported compilation error. Additionally, there are potential runtime risks in `wenfa.cpp` (e.g., accessing `tks[i+1]` or `tks[i+2]` without bounds checking), but the immediate blocker is the struct redefinition.
