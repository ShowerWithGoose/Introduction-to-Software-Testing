### Summary of the Issue
The program fails with a **Compilation Error**. The root cause is a **naming conflict** in `Parser.java`. The local variable `preRead` is declared with the exact same name as the class method `preRead(int n)`. In Java, a local variable cannot share the same identifier as a method within the same scope. This causes the compiler to fail when it tries to resolve the method calls `preRead(1)` and `preRead(2)`, as it interprets `preRead` as the local variable instead of the method. This conflict occurs in both the `CompUnit()` and `Stmt()` methods.
