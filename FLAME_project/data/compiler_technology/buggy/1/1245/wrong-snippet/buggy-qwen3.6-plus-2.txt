    *   Since the Lexer runs completely before the Parser, all Lexer errors (like `18 a`) are added to the list before any Parser errors (like `16 j`).
    *   The `output` method in `Parser.java` (and `Lexer.java`) iterates through `Lexer.errorTokens` and writes them to `error.txt` without sorting them by line number.
    *   Therefore, the root cause is the lack of sorting by line number in the error output logic.
