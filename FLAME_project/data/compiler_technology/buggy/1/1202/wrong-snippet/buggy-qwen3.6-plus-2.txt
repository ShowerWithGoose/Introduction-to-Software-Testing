**Conclusion:**
The error is in `Parser.java`, specifically in the `parseStmt` method. The method calls `parseExp()` (which triggers error detection and logging) to lookahead/check for assignment, then backtracks using `backto(initPos)`, and then calls `parseExp()` again to actually parse the expression. Since the error logging has side effects (adding to `errorLogs`) that are not undone by `backto`, the same error is logged multiple times.

The fix should involve preventing error logging during the lookahead phase, or clearing/restoring the error log state, or restructuring the logic to avoid double parsing. Given the constraint to identify the location, I will point to the `parseStmt` method in `Parser.java`.
