          It does NOT print the `INTCON` token itself?

          `IntConst` prints `INTCON 0`.
          
          So `StmtExp` prints `INTCON 0 ... SEMICN`.
          And `Stmt` prints `<Stmt>`.

          `SEMICN ;`
