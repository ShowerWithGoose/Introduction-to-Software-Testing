The issue is that the error logs are being generated in the wrong order. The expected output is sorted by line number in ascending order, but the buggy output has line 18 before 16. This suggests that the errors are being recorded in the order they are encountered during parsing rather than being sorted by line number.

Looking at the code, the `ErrorLog.makelog_error()` method adds errors to a list in the order they occur, and `WriteErrorLogs()` writes them in that same order without sorting. However, the problem requires errors to be output "sorted by line number in ascending order".

The bug is in the `WriteErrorLogs` method in `ErrorLog.java` - it needs to sort the error logs by line number before writing them.
