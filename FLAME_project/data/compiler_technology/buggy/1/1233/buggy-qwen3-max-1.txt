However, looking more carefully at the problem, the real issue is that the parser is not detecting the missing right parentheses because it's not properly handling the error cases in function calls. But there's another critical issue:

In the StmtGetChar and StmtPrint classes, when they detect a missing right parenthesis, they add an error but don't consume the unexpected token, which can cause the parser to get stuck or skip subsequent parsing.

But the most fundamental issue is actually in the lexer's bounds checking. When checking `inputLine.charAt(positionNum)` after incrementing `positionNum`, there's no check to ensure `positionNum` is within bounds.
