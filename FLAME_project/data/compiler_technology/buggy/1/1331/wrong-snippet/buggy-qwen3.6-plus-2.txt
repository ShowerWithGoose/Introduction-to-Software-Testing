Based on the provided code and the problem description, there is a critical logical error in `include/LexType.h`. The mapping for brace tokens `{` and `}` to their corresponding `LexType` enum values (`LBRACE` and `RBRACE`) is swapped with the mapping for bracket tokens `[` and `]` (`LBRACK` and `RBRACK`).

Specifically:
- `{"{", LexType::LBRACK}` maps the left brace `{` to `LBRACK` (which should be `[`).
- `{"}", LexType::RBRACK}` maps the right brace `}` to `RBRACK` (which should be `]`).
- `{"[", LexType::LBRACE}` maps the left bracket `[` to `LBRACE` (which should be `{`).
- `{"]", LexType::RBRACE}` maps the right bracket `]` to `RBRACE` (which should be `}`).

This will cause the parser to misinterpret braces as brackets and vice versa, leading to syntax analysis errors or incorrect output.
