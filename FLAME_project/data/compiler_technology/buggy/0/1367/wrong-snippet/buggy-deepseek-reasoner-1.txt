The primary issue is that the code does not check `i < line.length()` before accessing `line.charAt(i)` in several places, especially after incrementing `i` inside the whitespace-skipping loop and in subsequent conditional checks. This will cause a `StringIndexOutOfBoundsException` when the line ends with whitespace or at the end of line, leading to the program crashing and producing no output.
